Pad Krapow

Pad Krapow, otherwise known as Pad Kra Pao, is a beloved stir-fry dish in Thailand that is just as delicious as it iseasy to make.

Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a Thai noodle stir fry with a sweet-savoury-sour sauce scattered with crushed peanuts. It’s made with thin, flat rice noodles, and almost always has bean sprouts, garlic chives, scrambled egg, firm tofu and a protein – the most popular being chicken or prawns/shrimp.

Traganou beach, Rhodes

The beach of Traganou is located 20 km south of Rhodes town and 4 km from Faliraki Beach. A superb cave is standing on one extremity of the beach, giving to the whole place a unique and magical atmosphere.

The Blue Eye, Cape Verde

Buracona, also known as The Blue Eye is one of the few tourist attractions of Cape Verde islands and Sal. A beautiful underwater cage turning magic blue and turquoise by the sunlight. Buracona and the “blue eye” is basically a natural pool formed by the ocean into the lava rocks. When the light hits this natural pool from the right angle, the color turns into a magic turquoise blue reviling "the blue eye". It is absolutely beautiful. You can swim in the natural lava pool right next to the “blue eye” but this can be dangerous on windy days.
